How the gauge works — two phases

One measures how much people care. The other reads what they argue.

Phase 1 · the money

Count the zaps

Sum the sats people zapped onto posts about a proposal — real money, already spent, straight off the relays. No AI. Every receipt is verified against the recipient’s own Lightning provider, so a forged zap can’t buy its way up.

NO LLM  /  ~250MS WARM  /  VALIDATED RECEIPTS
Phase 2 · the argument

Read the posts

An LLM reads every post found for a proposal, sorts each into good / not good / not sure, and writes back what both camps are actually arguing. The gauge is the net lean, neutrals excluded.

The data — 13 proposals, measured

Every number below was read from live Nostr. Phase 2 is the LLM’s net lean; Phase 1 attributes each real zap to the stance of the post it paid for.
